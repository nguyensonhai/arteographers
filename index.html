Welcome to ATG


Who are we?

We are a group of dancers coming from different countries, gathered together to create a playground for people who are deeply interested in dancing hiphop. We combine art and choreos together and hence, the name Arteographer.

Here are our founder and current leader of the team.
This is Bao Le, our kind founder of this circle since 2013, he has been dancing for most of his life and he has a lot of experience in dancing as well as inspiring others to put effort into this sport.

Bao currently is also having his own online class for dancing both for beginners and advance dancers due to the pandemic. If you are interested and in some circumstances can not attend our regular weekly open practices. Please follow this link to acquire further info of his classes and how to register.

This is Minh Kobe, our current leader, he may not look like one but he sure put a lot of effort into dancing and improved so much since the day he started joining us. He will help you guys carry on Bao Le's fire in this art.
